### Solve
<br>
```python
flag = "fl4g_i5_h3r3!"
data = "7D4D2344360276036F5B2F46761839".decode('hex')

m = 0

for c in flag:
	m = m ^ ord(c)

for c in data:
	flag += chr( m ^ ord(c) )
	m = m ^ ( m ^ ord(c) )

print flag
```
